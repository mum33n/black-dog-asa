    Why Algorand?

    Algo transaction finality is below 4.5 seconds which allows a user to send money to anyone, anywhere, anytime without delay.

    Transaction fees on Algo are by far the smallest. Just a fraction of a cent (~0.001 Algo). Algo was built for billions of users, so increase in traffic wont slow down payments. It is a scalable, secure and decentralized blockchain.
